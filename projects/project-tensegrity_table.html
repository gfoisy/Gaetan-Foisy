I made a floating table!

I saw a cool table. It was 3D printed and suspended by it's own weight.


The "Impossible Table"

I thought that this was a really cool concept and wanted to put my own spin on it. I knew that if I looked, I would find tons of other people's takes on this concept, so I made the decision: No Google. Well, not all of google, just no googling around for ideas. I was hoping that this would leave my relitively "un-tainted" so there would be a chance that I could stumble upon a completly original idea.


A special thanks to my dad for being patient when I "surprise borrowed" all of his tools and gave most of them back.
